Extra Notes-

Colour Scheme: As I wanted to enjoy this project as much as I could & to give it my full attention I needed to have a colour scheme that I thought was visually appealing but also easy to read and to understand that context or each section.

The colours that I used were Black(#000000), Orange(#cc7a00) & White(#ffffff)


Buttons: Every button on my CV has the background colour of orange with black text. As the user hovers over these buttons change colour to indicate that they are being hovered over. I chose to use a linear-gradient colour scheme when hovering over a button. The colour will start with black, fade to orange then fade back into black. In the education section each year that is displayed is a tab button that the user will need to hover over to see the information.


Parallax: I add the parallax static image when scrolling to the back ground of my CV website to give each section more depth and to also nicely layout each section of my website.


Links: There is a total of 8 link in my website. These links are as followed. The Github, Linkedin & Gmail icons in my fixed navigation bar, the TUDublin & Crumlin college buttons in my education section, & finally the 3 images in my work experience page that link to the companies sites. All these links open in separate tabs too.
